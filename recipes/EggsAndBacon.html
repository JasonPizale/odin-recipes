Eggs and Bacon

Quick and Easy Breakfast

This recipe is for a simple american breakfast consisting of eggs and bacon! A classic breakfast that everyone and their nan should know!

Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs
  • Bacon
  • Tbps of butter

Steps

  1. Alright ya bum, the first step is to fry the bacon in a pan over medium high heat until crispy. Flipping regularly and when needed. Put aside on a plate and paper towels to drain the excess oil.
  2. Use the same pan with the existing bacon grease to fry the eggs. Place over medium heat and crack both your eggs.
  3. Cook the eggs anyway you like them. For sunny side up - leave the eggs to fry on one side and cover the pan with a lid to allow the yolks to steam and cook. For over-easy eggs- flip the eggs over after a few minutes and cook for another minute.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste and enjoy!
